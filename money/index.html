How to Use It

Step by Step

1
Open the link above — it goes straight to the shared kitty. Bookmark it on your phone before you leave.
2
Enter your name the first time — KittySplit remembers you in the same browser after that.
3
Log expenses as they happen. Tap "Add expense," enter what you paid, who paid, and who it's split between.
4
Don't wait until the end. The longer you wait, the harder it is to remember who bought the third round of ouzo.
5
Settle up at the end of the trip. KittySplit calculates the minimum transfers — usually just 1–2 payments per person.
💡
Tip: Pre-trip costs (charter deposit, Airbnb, flights) are already paid and settled separately. Only log shared on-trip expenses — things bought for the group during the trip itself.
