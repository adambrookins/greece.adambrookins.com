Mandraki Harbor, Corfu Old Town
~8 NM · ~1.5 hrs · UNESCO heritage · first Med moor
Sailing

Day Highlights

  • 🏛 Corfu Old Town — UNESCO site. Venetian architecture, narrow lanes, incredible food.
  • 🏰 Palaio Frourio — Old Fortress with sweeping views of the Ionian.
  • ☕ Liston Promenade — tree-lined arcade for coffee, people-watching, and pretending you live here.
  • 🎭 Byzantine Museum, Venetian architecture throughout Old Town.

Day
3
Monday, June 15, 2026
Lakka, Paxos
~20 NM · ~2.5 hrs · crystal-clear anchorage · sea caves
Sailing

Day Highlights

  • 💎 Crystal-clear water. Paxos is famous for it. Actual swimming, not "we'll see it from the boat" swimming.
  • 🚣 Sea caves by kayak — rent in Lakka or use the boat's dinghy to explore the coastline.
  • 🏝 Optional: Antipaxos — anchor at Voutoumi or Vriki for snorkeling. The water is absurdly blue.
  • 🐙 Lakka tavernas for lunch and dinner. Fresh seafood. Grilled octopus mandatory.

Anchorage Details

MooringLakka Bay anchorage or town quay
Depth5–10m, sand & weed
ArrivalEarly — popular spot
Antipaxos altVriki / Voutoumi, 5–12m sand
🏴‍☠️ "Paxos has the clearest water in the Ionian. If you don't get in the water here, you have made a navigational error of life."

4
Tuesday, June 16, 2026
Parga, Mainland Greece
~22 NM · ~3 hrs · Venetian castle · colorful hillside
Sailing

Day Highlights

  • 🏰 Venetian Castle — panoramic views over Parga bay and the Ionian coast. Worth the climb.
  • 🏘 Colorful hillside town — orange and terracotta houses cascading to the water.
  • 🏊 Swim to Panagia Island chapel — tiny chapel on a tiny island right in the bay. Very "Greece postcard."
  • 🍷 Vibrant nightlife — Parga has good bars and restaurants right on the waterfront.

Sailing

Thursday, June 18, 2026
Plataria Marina
~8 NM · ~1.5 hrs · quiet fishing village · authentic
Sailing

Day Highlights

  • 🎣 Authentic Greek waterfront. No tourists, just locals and tavernas that have been there forever.
  • 🐟 Local seafood. Catch-of-the-day straight from the boats that were out that morning.
  • 😌 Relaxed pace. The anti-Mykonos. Sit, eat, watch the sun go down. Nothing scheduled.
  • 🌅 Last full evening in mainland Greece. Good night to sit in the cockpit with wine.
