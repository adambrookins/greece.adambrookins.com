13 Days · Boat + City + Italy

The Packing List

Sailing for 7 days, then two Italian cities. Pack light — a catamaran has storage, not a warehouse. Soft bags only on the boat.

📄 Download Printable PDF

🏴‍☠️ The Pirate's Packing Manifesto

A real pirate travels light. You have three contexts: (1) Athens sightseeing in June heat, (2) 7 days on a 45ft catamaran where everything gets wet and salty, and (3) two Italian cities where you will judge and be judged. Pack for all three. Soft-side bags are recommended for sailing — hard shell rollers may be able to be checked at the marina while we sail, but plan accordingly.

Bag Rules

What You Can Actually Bring

⛵
On the catamaran: Soft bags only — no hard-shell rollers. Bags live in your cabin. Think duffel or packable backpack. Rolling bags can be left at the marina for storage while we sail.

Sailing Essentials
Boat life · mandatory
✓
Soft duffel bag — no hard-shell rollers on the boat; rollers may be left at the marina for storage while we sail
✓
Non-slip boat shoes or old sneakers or go barefoot — no black soles on deck
✓
Flip flops / sandals — for ashore and swimming
✓
Swimsuits × 2–3 — you will swim every day
✓
Quick-dry towel — beach towel + Sunsail does provide a shower towel
✓
Rash guard / sun shirt — 7 hours on deck gets brutal
✓
SPF 50 sunscreen × plenty ⚠ no burns, jenny will get your back
✓
Polarized sunglasses — non-negotiable on open water
✓
Wide-brim hat or cap — your scalp will thank you
✓
Snorkel mask — Paxos and Sivota require it. Fins provided by Sunsail — just bring your mask.
✓
Light layers / fleece — nights on the water can be cool
✓
Sea sickness tablets — Bonine or Dramamine, bring both just in case. Day 4 is a 3-hour crossing.

Health & Safety
Don't skip any of these
✓

✓
Ibuprofen / paracetamol — boat headaches are a thing
✓

✓
After-sun lotion — aloe gel, the real stuff
